The Problem

Most people who want to learn coding give up within 2 weeks. Not because they are not smart — but because they hit a tutorial, get stuck, switch to another one, and end up going in circles. There is no shortage of content. There is a shortage of direction.

Our Fix

Avilor acts like a mentor. It figures out where you are, where you want to go, and maps out every step in between — with realistic timelines, real projects to build, and warnings about common traps. Then it breaks it down into daily focus so you always know what to do next.
