Avilor Updates

What's New

🧠

Smarter AI Prompting

Core

The AI now receives a detailed focus instruction built around your primary goal. Placement prep gets a DSA-heavy path. Startups get a ship-first structure. Job seekers get a portfolio + interview balance. Every roadmap is genuinely different based on WHY you're learning.

🎯

Goal-Driven Language Selection

AI

The AI now picks the right programming language for your exact goal. C++ for placements and interviews. Python for AI and data analysis. JavaScript for websites. Kotlin for Android apps. C# or C++ for games. One language used consistently through every phase, step, and task.

📋

Concrete Practice Tasks

Quality

Every task is now tiny, specific, and doable in 15-30 minutes. No more vague instructions like "practice variables". Instead you get exact tasks like "write a program that stores your name and age and prints them". You always know exactly what to build.

🔍

Better YouTube Search Queries

Resources

Every step now links to a highly specific YouTube search that always includes your chosen language. Instead of "loops tutorial" you get "python for loops beginner tutorial". You land on the right video faster with less guessing.

✅

Task Progress Saved

New

Your ticked tasks are now saved to the database. Close the tab, come back tomorrow, and your progress is exactly where you left it. When you advance to the next step, the slate clears automatically for the fresh tasks ahead.

🛡️

Secure Sessions

Security

Sessions are now signed with a secret key so your onboarding answers stay safe while you log in. All protected routes also check your token properly and redirect to login if it has expired, instead of crashing.

⚡

Smarter Error Handling

Stability

If the AI is temporarily unavailable, you now see a friendly message instead of a crash page. Your onboarding answers are preserved so you can retry without starting over. The AI call also has a 60-second timeout to prevent infinite hangs.
